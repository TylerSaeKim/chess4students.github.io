Lesson Plans

We will be offering multiple lesson types which are subject to change as we evaluate interest in this program.


Beginner Class

We will teach students in this class how to play chess and teach them the basics of strategic play

Intermediate/Advanced Class

We will teach students strategic play during a chess game in this class. We will also pair up students of similar levels to play against each other.
